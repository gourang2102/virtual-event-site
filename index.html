ABOUT HACON 2020

HACON 2020, a conference and step ahead in the cyber world for newbies presented by HAC-security. This conference is organized with a purpose to introduce newbies to the next level of cybersecurity. Participants will get the chance to represent their skill sets in front of cybersecurity experts via gaming sessions like CTF. With that, they will explore themselves with and to the cyber security community. Experts will discuss and share their work to the newbies.They will also get an interactive session with the expert to share their ideas and willingness to be part of this community. Winners will get the awards and certification of recognition in the community.

Community Partners

CTF-CHALLENGE

CTF(capture the flag), a hunting series of challenges made for security researchers to prove the best of their skill by participating in it.
CTF is like a game, participants have to solve cybersecurity based puzzles and exercises within the given time frame to gain points.
Participants can join it individually or with a team.
